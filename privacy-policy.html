Privacy Policy

AI Reminder

Published by AI Reminder Team · Last updated: October 12, 2025 (UTC)

This Privacy Policy describes how AI Reminder Team (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information when you use the mobile application AI Reminder (“App”). By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

1) Information We Collect

  • Account data (optional): If you choose to sign in, we may collect a display name and email address.
  • Usage & diagnostics: Anonymous crash logs, basic analytics (e.g., feature usage, device type, OS version).
  • Advertising data: If the App shows ads via Google AdMob, Google may collect identifiers and device information to deliver and measure ads. You can control personalized ads in your device settings.
  • Reminders content: Reminder titles, times and schedules are stored locally on your device unless you explicitly enable cloud sync features.

2) How We Use Information

  • Provide core features (creating, scheduling, and triggering reminders/notifications).
  • Improve performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • Show ads and measure their performance (if AdMob is enabled).

3) Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. Limited data may be shared with service providers strictly to operate the App:

  • Google AdMob (ads). See Google Privacy Policy and AdMob Policy.
  • Google Firebase (optional analytics/crash reporting). See Firebase Privacy & Security.

4) Data Storage & Security

  • Reminder data is stored on your device by default.
  • When cloud services are enabled, data may be transmitted securely and stored by the relevant provider.
  • We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data; however, no method is 100% secure.

5) Your Choices

  • Control ad personalization in Android settings: Settings → Google → Ads.
  • Grant/deny runtime permissions (e.g., notifications, calendar access).
  • Delete local app data via Settings → Apps → AI Reminder → Storage → Clear data.
  • Contact us to request deletion of server‑stored data (if any cloud features are enabled).

6) Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

7) International Transfers

If we process data outside your country, we will rely on appropriate safeguards as required by applicable laws.

8) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will post the new Policy on this page and update the “Last updated” date above.
