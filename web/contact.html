Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click the "Sign In" button in the app, select "Email Sign-In", and use the password reset option. A reset link will be sent to your email.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! The app works offline with local storage. Your tasks will sync to the cloud when you're back online and signed in.

How does family sharing work?

Sign in with your account, go to Settings, and tap "Family Sharing". Add family members by email, then select which tasks to share with them.

Which AI providers are supported?

We support Google Gemini (default), OpenAI, DeepSeek, and Qianwen. You can configure your preferred provider in Settings.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings and tap "Export Data" to download all your tasks and reminders.

Is my data secure?

Yes! All data is transmitted over secure HTTPS connections and stored using industry-standard security practices. Read our Privacy Policy for details.
